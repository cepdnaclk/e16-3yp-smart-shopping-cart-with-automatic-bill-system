SMART SHOPPING CART WITH AUTOMATIC BILL SYSTEM.

Computer Engineering Third year project - Semester 5 -GROUP 11

Project Idea

Cashier takes time to billing ,so long queues at the counters in supermarkets. So that shoppers have to wait lot of time near the counter.We have come up with a innovative solution for this problem.


SOLUTION:



Introducing smart shopping cart with automatic bill system

  • The shopper just have to scan the item before adding it into the cart.
  • This cart carries its own scanning device as a part of the cart.
  • Then the billing will be done automatically.
  • So that customer can pay the bill at the dedicated exit.
  • Shopper can't put an item without scanning ,it will be monitered in our system.
  • Shopper can remove any item ,the item will be removed from the bill also in our system
  • Also we provide an app, compatiable with the cart which contains many facilies like peying the bill,check shopping history.

Services we offer

We offer many facilities for the customers through our smart cart and featured mobile app.


Smart Cart :

  • Cart automatically calculates the bill of the current items inside it.
  • The bill is shown in a lcd touch screen .
  • You can connect your cart with the mobile app by scanning the QR code on the cart .
  • Cart automatically catches if you done a mistake when adding an item to the cart or removing from the cart.
  • Shopper can't put an item without scanning ,it will be monitered in our system.

Mobile app :

  • Customer User Account
  • Price lists
  • To-buy lists/online ordering
  • Discounts/Promotions notifications
  • Recent shopping activities
